About

Earthworm and Clesh joined forces in 2010 and have been making the best hip-hop albums this side of the mississippi ever since. The duo lives in Ogden, Utah and help keep the scene there alive drawing crowds of excited fans to see their show as well as promoting and colaborating with other musicions and artist from the area. They have made a unique music scene that is hard to find anywhere else and if you are ever in Ogden they would love to share it with you and tell you all about whats going on. Check out the links at the bottom listen to the tunes, and if you enjoy email the worm to send you a CD so you can bump it in your ride and share with your friends.
